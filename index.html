Our Story

Cascarino's Pizzeria was founded in 1989 by Giuseppe and Maria Cascarino, who brought their family's traditional pizza recipes from Naples, Italy to the heart of Manhattan. What started as a small neighborhood pizzeria has grown into a beloved NYC institution, but our commitment to authenticity and quality has never wavered.

Every pizza that leaves our wood-fired oven is crafted with the same attention to detail, passion, and pride that Giuseppe instilled in our recipes decades ago. We source only the finest Italian ingredients, including San Marzano tomatoes, buffalo mozzarella, and imported oils. Our dough is prepared daily using traditional methods—never rushed, never compromised.

Today, Cascarino's remains a family-run business, with the third generation continuing the legacy. We're not just serving pizza; we're sharing a piece of Italian culture, one authentic experience at a time.
