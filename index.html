About Cascarino's

Warm Hospitality, Bold Flavor

At Cascarino's Brick Oven Pizzeria & Ristorante in Whitestone, we bring together traditional Italian cooking with the authentic heat of a wood-burning brick oven. From handcrafted pizzas to classic entrees, every dish is made fresh and served with care.

Our passion is simple: exceptional food, welcoming atmosphere, and genuine Italian hospitality. Whether you're grabbing a slice or settling in for a full meal, we treat you like family.
